01 — Program

Accelerator
Program

We run a structured program for early- to mid-stage defence and dual-use startups in collaboration with key stakeholders from Finland, Ukraine and NATO countries.

We welcome Finnish, European and Ukrainian companies. Our program runs in parallel with Defence Builder — the #1 Ukrainian defence tech accelerator.

We will change the way startups do product development with feedback loops to real battlefields. This is your opportunity to validate, develop and test your products where they are intended to be used.

01

Connections

We act as a bridge builder between different defense forces and alliances enabling rapid innovation, collaboration, and deployment of critical defense technologies. 17Tech is collaborating with NATO and Ukrainian accelerators, investors, decisionmakers and front-line operators.

02

Battlefield Development

We provide direct access to testing, validation and development opportunities close to the battlefield through our Ukrainian partners. We support you in "Organic Product Development" methodology — fast iteration cycles from the Ukrainian battlefield.

03

Funding

We provide up to €40k to companies that take part in our program, in exchange for 4–12% of shares. We will also help you find further funding from our extensive network of investors.

04

Mentorship

World-class R&D professionals from top-tier research institutes; product leaders and engineers from global tech companies; defense professionals, battlefield operators, and procurement experts. Startups get weekly 1-on-1s throughout the program.

05

Demo Day

The accelerator culminates in a final Demo Day, where teams present to a curated group of investors, defense buyers, integrators, and military leadership. Each team walks away with field validation, investor visibility, and direct contacts with defense forces who can support operational adoption and deployment beyond the program.
